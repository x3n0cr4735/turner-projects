⬡ GROUND SYSTEMS

Magnetic Elevator

DESIGNATION: ME-2025

A maglev elevator system using opposing magnetic poles on opposite walls to levitate and guide the car. Computer-controlled with linked button interface. Eliminates cables and mechanical wear.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
■North/South magnet arrangement for stable levitation
■Motor-driven pull system at top
■Computerized control panel
■Frictionless magnetic guidance

△ AIR SYSTEMS

T-47/M

DESIGNATION: T-47/M

An improvement on NASA's oblique wing concept—adding a second wing for enhanced stability and lift distribution. Wide body design maximizes passenger capacity while the swept configuration minimizes wave drag at supersonic speeds.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
■Dual oblique wing configuration
■Wide-body fuselage for passenger capacity
■Smooth glide characteristics at takeoff
■Supersonic cruise capability

◇ SPACE SYSTEMS

E-109

DESIGNATION: E-109

A hybrid spaceplane with retractable wings for atmospheric flight and a detachable rocket stage for orbital insertion. Three main engines provide boost in atmosphere before rocket section separates for space operations.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
■Three atmospheric boost engines
■Retractable wing system (in/out)
■Detachable rocket stage
■Guided auxiliary systems for space ops

AV-14 "Toxic"

DESIGNATION: AV-14

A manned submersible designed for deep-sea mining operations. Features reinforced pressure vessel, robotic extraction arms, and mineral transport tubes to surface. Modular drilling head with terrain-adaptive legs.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
■Pilot life support with oxygen system
■Robotic claw/arm for extraction
■Mineral transport tube to surface
■Adaptive leg system for terrain navigation
